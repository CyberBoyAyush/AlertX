Safety Precautions

Stay safe by following these essential precautions during a disaster.

During a Flood

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Stay tuned to emergency broadcasts for updates.
  • Turn off gas and electricity if instructed.

During an Earthquake

  • Drop, cover, and hold on until the shaking stops.
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects.
  • If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings.
  • Check for injuries and hazards after the shaking stops.

During a Fire

  • Evacuate immediately if instructed.
  • Use the stairs, not elevators, during evacuation.
  • If trapped, stay low to the ground and cover your nose and mouth.
  • Check doors for heat before opening them.
